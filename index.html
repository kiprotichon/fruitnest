Connecting Kenya's Harvest to the World

At FruitNest Africa, we aggregate high-quality fruits like avocados, mangoes, and passion fruits directly from trusted farmers across the Kerio Valley and surrounding regions. Our mission is to supply clean, traceable, and export-ready produce to local markets and international buyers, ensuring fair prices for farmers and premium quality for consumers.
